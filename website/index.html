Why this course is different

🎛️ Play first, formalize second

Every concept starts as an interactive widget you can poke. Only after your hands understand it does the math arrive — and by then it's just naming what you already saw.

⛏️ You write the quantum code

Notebooks give you the scaffolding; you fill the physics. Instant checkers tell you not just that you're wrong, but why — and honest shot noise never fails a correct answer.

🎨 Analogies from your world

Cricket fan? Baker? Guitarist? The Analogy Studio generates AI prompts with the precise physics baked in, so your favorite AI explains each concept through your life — without the pop-science myths.
